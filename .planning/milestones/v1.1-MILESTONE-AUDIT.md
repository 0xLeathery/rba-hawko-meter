### Phase 8: ASX Futures Live Data

- ASX scraper intentionally excluded from `pipeline/main.py` orchestrator — runs via dedicated `daily-asx-futures.yml` CI job. Manual `workflow_dispatch` of weekly pipeline does not refresh ASX data; it re-reads existing CSV. By design, not a functional gap.

### Phase 10: NAB Capacity Utilisation Gauge

- `business_confidence.data_date` in `status.json` shows `2026-03-31` (Q1 end) due to `resample_to_quarterly('QE')` mapping January 2026 data to quarter-end date. The "Data as of" footer shows a future date. Cosmetic — staleness threshold (45 days) fires correctly when data genuinely ages.
